How It Works

Simple, effective, automatic.

1

Install & Grant Permission

Download CatPaws and grant Input Monitoring and Accessibility permissions in System Settings. This allows the app to detect keyboard patterns.

2

Cat Approaches Keyboard

When your cat steps on the keyboard, CatPaws detects the characteristic pattern of multiple adjacent keys being pressed simultaneously.

3

Keyboard Locks Instantly

CatPaws blocks all keyboard input and shows a notification. Your work is safe from random cat-generated text.

4

Cat Leaves, You Continue

When the keys are released, CatPaws is unlocked with a mouse click or 5 presses of the ESC key. Resume your work as if nothing happened.

Using CatPaws

A quick guide to get you started.

üöÄ First Launch & Setup

When you first launch CatPaws, you'll see the onboarding screen. Grant Input Monitoring and Accessibility permissions in System Settings to allow CatPaws to detect keyboard patterns. The app will guide you through the process.

üìç Menu Bar Icon

CatPaws lives in your menu bar as a small paw icon. The icon shows you the status: filled paw means active, empty paw means idle and paw with an X means missing permissions. Click it to access settings, view statistics, or quit the app. The icon changes to show when the keyboard is locked.

üîí When Cat Detected

When CatPaws detects a cat on your keyboard, it shows a friendly notification and locks keyboard input. Click the notification or press ESC 5 times in a row to unlock your keyboard.
